Al-Balad Historic Jeddah is home to buildings and archaeological landmarks, and residents call it “Al-Balad,” meaning because it is located in the center of the city, Jeddah, and it carries within it historical stories dating back to pre-Islamic.

Riyadh Season

Riyadh Season is an international entertainment festival held in the city of Riyadh, the capital of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and aims to transform it into a global tourist entertainment destination, in accordance with the objectives of the Quality of Life Program, one of the programs of Saudi Vision 2030.

Jeddah Season

The Jeddah Season Festival is a season that aims to highlight Jeddah, include it in the global community, and make it the capital of tourism and entertainment in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The festival includes huge events in a world of entertainment, including music, art, culture, sports and more.

Taif Season

Taif Season is one of the Saudi seasons launched by the Saudi Commission for Tourism and National Heritage, and aims to highlight the cultural, tourism and historical components of the city of Taif. Its activities varied between sports, entertainment, culture, arts, and others.

Aseer Season

The Aseer season is considered one of the most important tourist seasons in the Kingdom, as the region is characterized by its picturesque nature and charming mountain scenery. The season attracts thousands of visitors annually, especially during the spring.

NEOM City

NEOM is a futuristic city located on the northwestern coast of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and is part of the “Kingdom’s Vision 2030” project, which aims to diversify the Kingdom’s economy and develop the tourism and entertainment sector. It is one of the largest giant projects in the world.
