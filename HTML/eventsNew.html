Jeddah

The city of Jeddah is one of the governorates of the Makkah Al-Mukarramah region, located in the west of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia on the Red Sea coast. It is considered the economic and tourist capital of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Al-Riyadh

Riyadh is the capital of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, its largest city and the third largest Arab capital in terms of population. Riyadh is located in the middle of the Arabian Peninsula on the Najd Plateau, at an altitude of 600 meters above sea level.

Al-Ula

Al-Ula is one of the cities of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, located in the northwest of the Kingdom, and administratively affiliated with the Emirate of the Medina region, and it is one of the most important tourist destinations in Saudi Arabia.

Al-Taif

Taif Season is one of the Saudi seasons launched by the Saudi Commission for Tourism and National Heritage, and aims to highlight the cultural, tourism and historical components of the city of Taif. Its activities varied between sports, entertainment, culture, arts, and others.
